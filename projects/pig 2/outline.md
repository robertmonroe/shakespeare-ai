# Book Summary
"Arnie's City Shuffle" is a heartwarming short story about Johnny, a city boy longing for something more than his mundane life. His wish is unexpectedly granted when he encounters Arnie, a dancing pig who has escaped from a nearby farm. The two quickly become best friends, but Johnny knows his parents wouldn't approve of keeping a pig in their apartment. He hides Arnie, leading to increasingly complicated and humorous situations.

The story escalates when Johnny's parents discover Arnie, leading to heartbreak and a desperate runaway adventure. Johnny, determined to keep his best friend, flees the city with Arnie. A city-wide search ensues, causing panic for Johnny's parents. The story culminates with a joyful reunion and a surprising revelation: Johnny's parents planned to move to a farm so Johnny and Arnie could live together. The story celebrates the unexpected bonds of friendship and the importance of family.
