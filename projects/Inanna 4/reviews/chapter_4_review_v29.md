### 4. Redundancy

*   **Redundancy in Crash Report:** The crash report appears twice, once immediately after the crash sequence and again as its own scene opener. This feels repetitive.

### 5. Flow and Transitions

*   **Scene 5 Disruption:** Scene 5, Enlil's report, interrupts the flow of Inanna's rescue and healing. Logically, it makes sense for Enlil to deal with the pod loss before Inanna has fully recovered, but dramatically, it breaks the tension and momentum building around her relationship with Enkidu. Moving it to the end of the chapter, after the healing and the week time jump, would improve the pacing and give more weight to the relationship being formed.
