## "The Serpent's Kiss" - Chapter 1 Review

### 1. Internal Consistency

*   **Issue 1**: In Scene 1, M refers to the theft as "lifted from its secure facility in Salisbury." In Scene 1, M says "scientists involved are…deceased," then she says attributed to a laboratory accident. That is a logical contradiction. If scientists were directly involved in the theft, a lab accident would not be a suitable cover story.
*   **Issue 2**: In Scene 1, M says, "Find Martel. Find the weapon. Neutralize the threat. Do you understand?" In Scene 2, Bond's Rolex vibrates discreetly with coded information regarding the cards. This seems unnecessary to a 00X in the field if the objective is to find the weapon. 00X should be more focused on surveillance of Martel.
